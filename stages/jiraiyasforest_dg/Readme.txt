My 9 year old son made a beautiful drawing (Manga style) of Jiraiya's forest from the anime Naruto.
I liked the draw so much that I decided to make a stage for Mugen in HD.

Credits:
--------

OldGamer: Thanks for technically tweaking the scenario for me.

beterhans: Created a tutorial to make a widescreen version of the scenarios without having to modify the Mugen configuration.


Music:
------

Jiraiya's Theme - Naruto Original Soundtrack 3 - by Toshio Masuda & MUSASHI PROJECT
℗ 2005 Aniplex Inc. - Released on: 2005-04-27

---

This scenario was made in 2 versions: Mugen's standard resolution (4:3) and widescreen (16:9). Choose the .def file according to your preference.

---

Install stage: Place the stage in the "stages" folder. (choose if it will be with the directory or without it - you must inform the correct path in select.def) and the music in the "sound" folder.
